Welcome to MaxKanji

A simple Kanji, Jukugo, and Tango Reference Site!

A simple self resource for 漢字

I'm currently a software engineer. I specialize in Web, Mobile, and 3D technologies, but apart from that I have a huge love for foreign languages. Even though I try very hard to get my foreign language studies in every day, the busy life of a young professional can take a toll on your time for dedicated and focused review. So instead of fighting the day to find time between writing programs and keeping up with my languages, I decided to merge the two! Why not right?

As you can see there are not that many Kanji out there in the database but I will be adding more and more every day and week. My goal is to get 20-30 out every week. Thus, by the end of February I should have all the 常用漢字 in this database!
